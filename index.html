An interactive installation based on audience's motion

Keyword: interactive art, real-time render, particle art


Year:2022

SUBSTANCES is an interactive installation made for UCSD ICAM SENIOR EXHIBITION. The installation is consist of four parts, Fire, Earth, Water, and Air, which are the four basic elements in the Ancient alchemy.

background information

According to Alchemy, the world is made up of four elements, Air, Earth, Water and Fire. Air represents the ability to guide and pull everything, Earth represents the vitality that can nourish life, Water represents the fluidity and uncertainty of things, and Fire represents the powerful destructive and reshaping power. The whole installation will be divided into four modes to present the different characteristics of the four elements. In each mode, viewers can interact with the canvas through their motions.

SUBSTANCES(2022) will be on display at the adam d. kamil gallery (la jolla, ca) from june 11-13, 2022.
